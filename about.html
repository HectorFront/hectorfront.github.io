About me

Hello!

I'm Hector, Front-end Engineer at the @FullCam team. In 2021, I founded @Mexpenses with just an idea to make my daily finances easier. Since then, it has become something of extreme importance to my users and to me today. Since 2019, I have 5 years of professional experience, always seeking to learn, improve, and learn about new technologies in the market. My role as a developer in these companies is to create innovations, intuitive experiences that have a great impact on users, and solve problems from the easiest to the most difficult. I started programming at the age of 16 in a school with a technical course in System Development in the city of Garça SP. I fell in love with learning web-oriented languages and UX/UI development tools. During my studies, I started my internship at @Fulltime through the recommendation of a teacher. There, I had the opportunity to start my career and become a full-time qualified professional.

Lately, I've been very involved with frameworks and libraries like Next.js, React.js, TypeScript, Vitest and other tools that help with the productivity of web development. I have intermediate experience with cloud service providers like AWS (Amazon Web Services), building API's RESTful with MySQL database, and Microservices using Node.js and Python.

I like to spend my time playing online games, watching suspense movies on streaming services, and always looking for new places to enjoy different flavors. If you want to talk to me, I'm open to new challenges and opportunities so that I can evolve and contribute more and more. Feel free to send me an email at hectddos103@gmail.com or connect with me on LinkedIn.
